Clean toilet
Pour toilet bowl cleaner in the toilet bowl.  Use a toilet brush and clean under the inside rim and all inside bowl surfaces.  Flush toilet.  Wet paper towel and put cleaner on towel.  Wipe down all surfaces on the outside of the toilet.
