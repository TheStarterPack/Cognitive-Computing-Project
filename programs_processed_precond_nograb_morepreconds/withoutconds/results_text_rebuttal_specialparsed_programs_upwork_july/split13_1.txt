Get glass of milk
Get an empty glass. Take milk from refrigerator and open it. Pour milk into glass.


[Walk] <kitchen> (1)
[Walk] <glass> (1)
[Find] <glass> (1)
[Grab] <glass> (1)
[Walk] <fridge> (1)
[Open] <fridge> (1)
[Find] <milk> (1)
[Grab] <milk> (1)
[Pour] <milk> (1) <glass> (1)
[PutObjBack] <milk> (1)
[Close] <fridge> (1)
